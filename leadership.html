Leadership & Vision

Abrar Asif

Founder & Owner, Toruk Makto Golf League

Abrar Asif is the foundational visionary behind Toruk Makto, Pakistan's historical first commercial corporate golf league framework. Driven by a commitment to modernize the domestic sporting paradigm, he successfully bridged premium commercial corporate groups with elite athletic performance pipelines across Pakistan's iconic clubs.

An active competitive single-digit handicapper himself, Abrar understands the deep mechanical, institutional, and environmental needs of professional players. Through strategic high-profile structural design, he built a unified matrix that empowers local golf clubs, anchors national multi-platform sports visibility networks, and crafts historic growth blueprints for upcoming generations of competitive athletes across the country.
