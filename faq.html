Frequently Asked Questions

Toruk Makto Golf League is Pakistan's premier corporate golf league, bringing together elite teams for competitive tournament play at the highest standards.

Companies can register teams by visiting our Contact & Registration page and submitting the team registration form. Limited slots are available each season.

Players must be members of the representing corporate organization with valid golf handicap credentials. Professional and amateur golfers are welcome.

Official venues are: DAC Golf Club & Karachi (DAC&GC) and Airmen Golf Club, Karachi. These premium courses provide world-class facilities for championship-level competition.

Scoring includes both Gross Score and Net Score calculations based on player handicaps. Teams compete on both combined and individual performance metrics.
